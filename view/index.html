PLEP Game 2026

Use WASD keys to move the character around the canvas.

Controls

  • W - Move Up
  • A - Move Left
  • S - Move Down
  • D - Move Right
  • E - Pick Up Item (when near shelf)

Objective

Collect the 3 items shown in the order list (top-left of canvas). Walk near shelves and press E to pick up items.
