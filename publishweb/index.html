PROJECT RUNNER

Use WASD keys to move the character around the canvas.

Controls

  • W - Move Up
  • A - Move Left
  • S - Move Down
  • D - Move Right
  • E - Pick Up Item (when near shelf)
  • F - Fulfill an order when near a vehicle
  • Q - Drop an unwanted item from the current inventory slot
  • 1-6 - Select inventory slot
  • Esc - Pause Game (shift timer still runs!)

Objective

Collect items. Walk near shelves and press E to pick up items. Be as accurate as you can to keep the boss happy. Work quickly and meet your quota!
